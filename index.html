About The Event

About The Event

Join us on a transformative journey at our job fair, 'Unlocking Potential: Paving the Path to Inclusive Employment.' We celebrate diversity and firmly believe in the untapped potential of every individual, regardless of their abilities. Our goal is to spotlight the unique skills of Persons with Disabilities (PWDs) and actively break down workforce barriers. Together, we promise a brighter, more inclusive future where everyone can thrive and contribute to society, irrespective of their abilities.
