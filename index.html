About The Event

Join us on a transformative journey at our job fair, 'Unlocking Potential: Paving the Path to Inclusive Employment.' We celebrate diversity and firmly believe in the untapped potential of every individual, regardless of their abilities. Our goal is to spotlight the unique skills of Persons with Disabilities (PWDs) and actively break down workforce barriers. Together, we promise a brighter, more inclusive future where everyone can thrive and contribute to society, irrespective of their abilities.

FAQ

  • What is a MargaDarshan job mela?

    MargaDarshan Job Mela is a unique job fair tailored to connect well-trained candidates with disabilities (PWDs) with promising employment opportunities. It is an initiative of WinVinaya Foundation, a non-profit organization that works to empower PWDs through education, employment, and advocacy.

  • Who is eligible to attend a MargaDarshan job mela?

    MargaDarshan job mela are open to PwD candidates who are trained by WinVinaya and seeking employment. This includes people with physical, sensory, intellectual, or mental disabilities.

  • How to participate in MargaDarshan job mela?

    Inclusive Companies: Connect with our placement team (placement@WinVinayaFoundation.org) to understand our trained candidate's pool, get sensitized, do the job mapping prior to the event day.
    PWD Candidates: Candidates who are trained by WinVinaya ONLY can participate in this job mela. Connect with our sourcing team for more information (sourcing@WinVinayaFoundation.org)

  • As a student, how can I prepare for a MargaDarshan job mela?

  • What should I expect at a MargaDarshan job mela?

    When you arrive at a MargaDarshan job mela, you will be greeted by volunteers who can help you navigate the event. You will also have the opportunity to meet with potential employers, like-minded companies, network with other PwDs/organizations, and learn about assistive devices, resources and hold hands to create an inclusive world.

  • What types of jobs are available at a MargaDarshan job mela?

    We are connecting with wide range of inclusive companies who are from MNCs, MSMEs and Start-ups. Depending upon the industry need and our candidate's skill sets, we map the candidates prior to the event for the smooth interview process. Job roles will include IT and Non-IT.

  • What should I bring to a MargaDarshan job mela?

    You should bring your positivity, enthusiasm, and your learning mind. If you are a candidate, please bring your updated resume, a list of references, and a copy of your disability certification. You may also want to bring a portfolio of your work, if applicable.
